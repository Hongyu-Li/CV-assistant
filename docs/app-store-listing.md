## 3. Description

### English

CV Assistant is a professional desktop application designed to streamline the creation of high-quality resumes using advanced artificial intelligence. By combining your personal profile with specific job descriptions, the app generates tailored CVs that highlight your most relevant qualifications.

The application supports 12 major AI providers, giving you the flexibility to use your preferred models:

- OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, DeepSeek
- Ollama (for local models), OpenRouter, Groq
- Mistral, Qwen, Zhipu, Kimi, and Custom API endpoints

Key Features:

- **AI Generation**: Automatically draft professional resumes based on your experience and target job requirements.
- **Rich Markdown Editor**: A Tiptap-based editor providing a Typora-like live rendering experience for seamless formatting.
- **Profile Management**: Maintain a comprehensive personal profile with Markdown support to serve as the foundation for all your resumes.
- **Multi-language Support**: Generate CVs in English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, French, German, and Spanish.
- **Bilingual Interface**: Easily switch between English and Chinese UI.
- **Theme Options**: Support for Light, Dark, and System themes to suit your workspace preference.
- **Local Data Storage**: All your data, profiles, and generated resumes are stored 100% locally. No data is uploaded to our servers.
- **Data Portability**: Configurable local workspace with built-in data migration tools.

CV Assistant is built for privacy and performance, ensuring your professional data remains under your control at all times.

## 9. App Privacy Questionnaire Guidance

### Data Collection

- **Does this app collect data?**: No.
- **Guidance**: The app stores all user data (profiles, resumes, settings) locally on the user's device. No personal information or content is transmitted to the developer's servers.

### Third-Party AI API Usage

- **Declaration**: Users must provide their own API keys for third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic).
- **Privacy Note**: When a user initiates a resume generation, the relevant profile data and job description are sent directly to the selected AI provider via their official API. Users should refer to the privacy policies of their chosen AI providers (e.g., OpenAI's privacy policy).
- **App Store Response**: In the "Data Collection" section, you can state that the app does not collect data. However, if Apple asks about data sent to third parties, clarify that data is only sent to AI services explicitly configured and triggered by the user, and the developer does not have access to this data.

### Recommended Answers for App Store Connect:

1. **Data Collection**: Select "No, we do not collect data from this app" (unless you add analytics in the future).
2. **Third-Party Integration**: If asked about third-party service integration, specify that the app is a tool that connects to user-provided AI services, and no data is shared with the app developer.
